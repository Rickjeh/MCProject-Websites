About

BombsAway Mod is a Minecraft modification designed to bring explosive tactical fun into your singleplayer or multiplayer experience by enabling TNT deployment while mounted. Rather than placing TNT the traditional way, this mod allows players to drop armed TNT directly from a Happy Ghast—turning your new airborne companion into a mobile bombing platform.

When mounted on a Happy Ghast and holding TNT, simply right-click to launch a primed TNT with a short fuse, mimicking an airstrike-style effect. The mod requires a flint and steel in your inventory unless you’re in Creative mode, maintaining balance while keeping the carnage satisfying.
